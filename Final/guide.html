Marika, Queen of the Eternal
The queen and goddess who is central to the narrative, associated with the Elden Ring.
Radagon of the Golden Order
Radagon is the Elden Lord, a pivotal character in the story and the main antagonist in the final stages.
Ranni the Witch
A powerful witch and one of the key figures in Elden Ring, guiding the player towards a unique ending.
Gideon Ofnir, the All-Knowing
He is a pivotal scholar and leader of the Roundtable Hold, a major location for players.
Fia, Deathbed Companion
A mysterious woman who provides guidance to the player, linked with death and the deathbed.
The Lands Between
The vast world where the story of Elden Ring unfolds, a land between life and death.
Roundtable Hold
A safe hub where players can meet NPCs, gain guidance, and prepare for their journey.
The Erdtree
A giant, luminous tree that is a symbol of life, power, and the Elden Ring itself in the game.
Stormveil Castle
An ancient and ruined castle, once home to a powerful lord, now a dungeon full of enemies and mysteries.
Liurnia of the Lakes
A large, serene region with lakes and ancient ruins, serving as one of the major exploration areas in the game.
Margit, The Fell Omen
A formidable boss encountered early in the game, symbolizing the corrupted nature of the world.
Godrick the Grafted
The first demigod boss, ruling over Stormveil Castle, who has grafted numerous limbs to gain power.
Radhan, the Starscourge
A colossal warrior who wields great power and commands the stars, one of the game's most memorable fights.
Malenia, Blade of Miquella
One of the most challenging bosses in the game, known for her speed, deadly attacks, and devastating healing abilities.
Fire Giant
A massive, fiery beast encountered later in the game, symbolizing the destructive power of fire in the world.
Elden Beast
The final boss in the game, representing the culmination of the Elden Ring's fate and the world’s destruction.
The Elden Ring
The powerful, mystical object at the heart of the game’s narrative, representing order and balance in the world.
The Lands Between
The expansive world of Elden Ring, divided into regions, where the player explores, fights, and uncovers lore.
The Tarnished
The player character, an individual chosen to restore the Elden Ring and regain the grace of the Erdtree.
Shards of the Elden Ring
Powerful fragments of the Elden Ring that were scattered across the world, each with significant influence.
Elden Lord
A title given to those who achieve mastery over the Elden Ring, often seen as the ultimate goal of the Tarnished.
Resting Places
Safe locations where players can restore their health, replenish resources, and level up their character.
Weapon Styles
The different types of combat techniques that players can use depending on the weapons they wield.
Great Runes
Special relics that grant immense power to those who possess them, often held by powerful bosses or demigods.
Grace
A divine force that guides the Tarnished on their journey and grants power to restore the Elden Ring.
Grace Sites
Special points scattered across the world where players can rest, fast-travel, and reflect on their journey.
Spirit Ashes
Items that allow players to summon allies and spirits to aid them in battle, enhancing gameplay strategy.
Colosseum
A battleground where players can face off in combat against both NPCs and other players in PvP duels.
Bloodborne Influence
References to the darker and more visceral combat and themes inspired by FromSoftware’s previous title, Bloodborne.
Runes
The in-game currency used to level up, buy items, and upgrade weapons; earned through defeating enemies.
The Great Tree
The central tree in the Lands Between, tied to the Elden Ring and the world’s balance, towering above all.
Golden Order
The guiding philosophy that governs the structure of the world and its laws, often associated with Radagon and Marika.
Shining Remains
Relics of the past, these mysterious glowing spots provide guidance and can reveal hidden lore or secrets.
Fractured Marika
A broken version of the goddess Marika, whose shattered influence creates chaos across the Lands Between.
